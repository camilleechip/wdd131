Overview

Purpose

I enjoy playing cozy video games in my free time. I like to relax and have a stress free time so I am making a blog about the best cozy/farm sim games that are available on Steam.

Audience

My audience is primarily adults who enjoy playing cozy games, but are not sure which ones are worth the time and money.

Dynamic elements

I am going to use an array to cycle through the available video games on the site. I also like the idea of a dark and light website so I will be adding that as well as buttons that will take the users to different pages.
